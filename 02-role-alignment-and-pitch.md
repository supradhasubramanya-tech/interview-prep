# Role Alignment And Pitch

## Your One-Minute Introduction

"I am an IT service delivery and digital platform leader with 14+ years of experience across customer-facing digital services, enterprise delivery, agile application management, and service governance. I have worked across business, product, engineering, operations, security, vendors, and customer-facing teams to turn stakeholder needs into roadmaps, backlogs, delivery plans, KPI visibility, and continuous improvements.

For Sandvik's Customer Portal, I see a business-critical service that needs reliability, strong governance, clear stakeholder alignment, and continuous improvement because it directly shapes how customers access parts, fleet information, manuals, warranty, and digital services. My strength is creating clarity across complex stakeholder environments while keeping focus on service value, user experience, and measurable outcomes."

That is exactly the kind of cross-functional ownership I enjoy."

## 30-60-90 Day Plan

First 30 days: learn and baseline
- Understand My Sandvik service boundaries, application landscape, suppliers, integrations, stakeholders, and decision forums.
- Review current SLAs, incidents, problem trends, support model, release model, security/compliance obligations, and roadmap.
- Meet business owners in Sales and Marketing, Parts and Services, Digital Mining, support, regional stakeholders, suppliers, and IT teams.
- Establish baseline dashboard: reliability, incidents, customer pain points, supplier performance, release predictability, adoption, and top risks.

Days 31-60: stabilize and improve
- Identify top 3 service pain points and create improvement actions with owners and dates.
- Strengthen governance cadence: roadmap intake, prioritization, risk review, supplier review, release readiness, and service review.
- Clarify SLA ownership, escalation paths, and operational readiness criteria.
- Review backlog quality and make sure business value, customer impact, security, and technical debt are visible.

Days 61-90: scale value
- Align the roadmap with measurable business and customer outcomes.
- Propose a service improvement roadmap for reliability, user experience, security, adoption, and supplier performance.
- Create a transparent reporting rhythm for stakeholders and leadership.
- Identify opportunities for self-service improvement, portal adoption, and reduced support dependency.

## Closing Statement

"The value I would bring is structured service ownership: making the Customer Portal reliable, measurable, well-governed, and continuously improved while keeping business, IT, suppliers, and customers aligned around the same priorities."
