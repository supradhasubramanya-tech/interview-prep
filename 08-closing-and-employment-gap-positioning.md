# Closing And Employment Gap Positioning

Use this if they ask about your current situation, availability, or why you are interested now. The goal is to be honest, calm, and confident. Do not apologize for not currently having a job.

## Main Principle

Do not say:

"Please give me a chance because I do not have a job."

Say instead:

"I am currently available, and I see that as a positive because I can commit fully and start with focus. What matters most to me is finding the right role where my service ownership, digital platform delivery, stakeholder coordination, and continuous improvement experience can create value."

## If They Ask About Your Current Situation

"At the moment I am not in a permanent role, so I am available to start sooner than someone with a long notice period. I have used this time to focus carefully on roles where my background is genuinely relevant. This Sandvik role stood out to me because it combines customer-facing digital services, IT service ownership, stakeholder alignment, supplier coordination, and continuous improvement."

## If You Want To Ask For A Fair Assessment

"I understand that my job titles have included Program Manager, Product Owner, and Service Delivery Lead rather than only Service Owner. But the responsibilities I have carried are very close to this role: service governance, customer-facing platforms, SLA/KPI visibility, incident and escalation handling, stakeholder coordination, supplier collaboration, roadmap alignment, and continuous improvement. I would appreciate being assessed on those examples and on how I would approach the Customer Portal service at Sandvik."

## Strong Closing Statement

"I am very interested in this role because I can see a clear match between what Sandvik needs and what I have been doing in practice: creating clarity, reliability, and measurable improvement across customer-facing digital services. If selected, I would bring commitment, structure, and a strong sense of ownership from day one."

## Shorter Closing Version

"I would really value the opportunity to contribute to Sandvik. I believe my experience is highly relevant to the Customer Portal service, and I am ready to bring focus, ownership, and measurable improvement to the role."

## Tone To Keep

- Confident, not pleading.
- Honest, not over-explaining.
- Focused on value, not personal difficulty.
- Available and motivated, not desperate.
- Specific to Sandvik and My Sandvik.
