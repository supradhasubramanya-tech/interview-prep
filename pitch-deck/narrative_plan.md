# Three-Slide Self-Pitch Deck Plan

Audience: Riitta Pernu, IT Delivery Manager, Sales and Marketing, and Lotta Amnebjer Nordqvist, Recruitment Specialist.

Objective: give Anuradha a concise, polished visual pitch for the IT Service Owner - Customer Portal interview. The deck should make her value clear in 3 to 5 minutes and support a conversation, not replace it.

Narrative arc:
1. Position Anuradha as an owner of business-critical customer-facing digital services.
2. Show the operating model she brings: transparency, data-driven decisions, accountability, and timely delivery.
3. Connect her experience directly to My Sandvik: reliable portal, stakeholder alignment, supplier governance, measurable customer value, and continuous improvement.

Slide list:
1. Service Ownership With Customer Value - introduces the headline pitch and the link between customer portals, reliability, and business value.
2. How I Create Impact - shows her four leadership/value pillars inspired by the earlier T-DAT example: transparency, data-driven decisions, accountability, and timeliness.
3. What I Bring To My Sandvik - maps her experience to the service owner outcomes Sandvik needs: stable service, aligned roadmap, supplier follow-up, security awareness, and measurable improvement.

Source plan:
- Use the existing Sandvik job description and interview-prep notes.
- Use the previous T-DAT/DRIVE-FT deck only as style inspiration: minimal slides, strong concept words, muted image plates, simple pillars.
- Do not reuse company logos or readable text from the source deck.

Visual system:
- Clean executive interview deck.
- Light backgrounds with charcoal text, Sandvik-inspired warm yellow accents, cool steel gray, and restrained green.
- Large headline typography and spacious layouts.
- Imagegen plates should be text-free, professional, and suitable as soft background/collage panels.

Imagegen plan:
- Generate one text-free 16:9 art-direction plate per slide.
- Slide 1: industrial customer portal ecosystem in a mining/engineering context, with calm blank space for headline text.
- Slide 2: cross-functional service governance and data visibility, with soft dashboard/team motifs and room for four editable value cards.
- Slide 3: global customer portal operations, uptime, integrations, and continuous improvement, with room for a 30-60-90 style outcome map.

Editability plan:
- All slide words, numbers, labels, callouts, and pillars must be editable PowerPoint text.
- Generated images are visual plates only.
- The final file should be an editable PPTX saved in this folder's outputs directory.
