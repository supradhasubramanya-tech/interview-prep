Interview: Monday, May 18, 2026 at 13:00 CEST on Microsoft Teams.

## Your Three Core Messages

1. I can own a business-critical customer-facing IT service end to end.
2. I create alignment across business, IT, engineering, security, suppliers, customers, and leadership.
3. I improve services with measurable governance, SLA/KPI visibility, agile delivery, and continuous improvement.

## Best Short Answer To "Tell Me About Yourself"

"I am an IT service delivery and digital platform leader with 14+ years of experience across customer-facing platforms, service operations, agile application delivery, and stakeholder governance. My strongest experience is creating clarity in complex environments: aligning business and IT stakeholders, managing roadmaps and backlogs, coordinating teams and suppliers, tracking SLAs and KPIs, and improving service performance. For Sandvik's Customer Portal, I see a critical digital service where reliability, customer experience, security, and business value all need to work together, and that is the kind of ownership I enjoy."

## Best Short Answer To "Why Sandvik?"

"Sandvik interests me because it combines industrial engineering, global scale, sustainability, and digital transformation in a very practical way. My Sandvik is directly connected to how customers access parts, fleet data, manuals, warranty, and digital services. That makes the Service Owner role meaningful because the service affects customer experience, uptime, aftermarket value, and trust."

## Best Short Answer To "Why Are You A Fit?"

"I bring the combination the role asks for: ITIL-based service management, customer-facing digital platform delivery, agile application management, stakeholder coordination, supplier collaboration, SLA/KPI reporting, governance, and continuous improvement. I have improved service request turnaround by 50 percent, improved delivery performance by 40 percent, managed platform delivery across 80+ engineers, and built dashboards for service transparency and decision-making."

## Salary Expectation Guidance

Use this as preparation, not as a rigid demand.

Sources checked:
- Unionen lists 2025 market salary for systemforvaltare and similar roles at SEK 38,430 to SEK 60,970 per month.
- Asta Agency summarizes SCB 2024 data for systemforvaltare at a median of SEK 50,600 per month, with 25th to 75th percentile at SEK 43,000 to SEK 59,100.
- A Statsskuld reposting of this exact Sandvik role estimated about SEK 52,600 per month, but this is a job-board estimate, not Sandvik's official salary.
- IT manager/IT-chef benchmarks are higher, but this role is service ownership rather than line IT management.

Suggested answer:

"Based on the scope of the role, the global service ownership responsibility, and my 14+ years of experience, my expectation is around SEK 63,000 per month gross fixed salary. I am open to discussing this depending on the full package, responsibility level, and benefits."

Flexible range:
- Conservative: SEK 58,000 to 62,000 per month.
- Balanced target: SEK 63,000 to 65,000 per month.
- Higher anchor if scope includes significant budget, supplier contract ownership, and global accountability: SEK 66,000 to 68,000 per month.

Do not apologize for the number. Say it calmly, then pause.
